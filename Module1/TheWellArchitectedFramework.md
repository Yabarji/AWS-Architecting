# AWS Well Architected Framework
[AWS Resource](aws.amazon.com/architecture/well-architected)

## Priorities
* Operational Excellence
    * The operational excellence pillar focuses on __running and monitoring systems__
* Security
    * Focuses on protecting information & systems. Key topics include confidentiality and integrity.
* Reliability
    * Ability to prevent and quickly recover from failures. Focused on __AVAILABILITY TO THE CUSTOMER__
* Performance Efficiency
    * Performance is a trade-off. Select the right resource types and sizes
* Cost Optimization
    * Cost Optimizzation focuses on avoiding unneeded costs. Key topics include understanding and controlling where money is being spent and selecting the most appropriate resources.
